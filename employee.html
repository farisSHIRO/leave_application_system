Total Annual Leave

  • - 8 days per year for employees who have worked 1-2 years

  • - 12 days per year for those who have worked 2-5 years

  • - 16 days per year for employees who have worked longer than 5 years

Format for Leave Reason

  • - Employee must have a reasonable excuse to apply for leave

  • - When filling up the form, please replace the space with '_'

  • - Example: Family_matters
